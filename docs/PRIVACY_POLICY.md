ONTrack – Privacy Policy
Last updated: June 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how ONTrack (“the App”) handles information when you use it. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Policy.

If you do not agree with this Policy, please do not use the App.

1. Information We Collect
1.1 Information You Provide
The App may collect information that you voluntarily provide, such as:

Data you enter into the App (e.g., notes, settings, preferences, or other content you choose to store in the app).

Feedback you send us by email or other support channels.

The exact types of data depend on how you use the App and which features you use.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information
Depending on configuration and platform services, the App may automatically collect:

Device information (e.g., device model, operating system version, language, and unique device identifiers).

Usage data (e.g., screens viewed, features used, timestamps, and basic interaction data).

Crash and performance data (e.g., error logs, stack traces, and diagnostic information to help us improve stability).

We do not intentionally collect information such as your name, email address, or contact list unless you explicitly provide it as part of support or account features.

1.3 Location Data
The App does not collect or store precise GPS location data unless explicitly stated in a separate permission prompt in the app. If such features are added in the future, they will only be used for the described purpose and subject to your consent.

2. How We Use Information
We use the information described above to:

Provide and operate the App and its features.

Maintain and improve performance, stability, and user experience.

Diagnose and fix crashes, bugs, and other technical issues.

Respond to user requests, questions, or support inquiries.

Analyze anonymized or aggregated usage patterns to understand how the App is used.

We do not sell your personal data.

3. Third‑Party Services
The App may use third‑party services (for example, analytics, crash reporting, or distribution platforms like Google Play) that may collect information used to identify you or your device. These services have their own privacy policies.

Examples of the kinds of services that may be used include:

Google Play (distribution, updates, basic device information and usage as part of Google’s own services).

Crash and analytics services (if enabled) to help us understand stability and usage trends.

Please refer to the privacy policies of any third‑party services used by the App for details on their data practices.

4. Data Storage and Retention
Data stored locally on your device remains on your device unless you choose to back it up or share it through other services.

If the App uses cloud sync or remote services in the future, data may be stored on secure servers managed by us or our service providers.

We retain data only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this Policy or as required by law. When data is no longer needed, we will take reasonable steps to delete or anonymize it.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure
We may share information in the following limited situations:

Service providers: With trusted third‑party providers who help us operate the App (e.g., analytics or crash reporting), only to the extent necessary for them to perform their services.

Legal requirements: When required to comply with applicable law, legal processes, or valid governmental requests.

Protection of rights: When necessary to protect our rights, property, safety, or that of users or the public.

We do not share your personal data with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

6. Your Choices and Controls
Depending on your device and platform, you may be able to:

Control app permissions (e.g., access to storage, notifications, or other device features) via your device settings.

Opt out of certain analytics or personalized services via system‑level privacy/ads settings or in‑app options (where available).

Delete local app data by clearing the app’s storage or uninstalling the App from your device.

If you contact us directly, you may request deletion or clarification of information you have provided, where technically feasible and not otherwise required to be retained by law.

7. Children’s Privacy
The App is not intended for children under 13 (or a higher age where required by local law), and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children in that age group. If we learn that we have inadvertently collected data from a child, we will take reasonable steps to delete it.

8. Security
We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information processed by the App against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
